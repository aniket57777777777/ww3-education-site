Introduction

This page presents a fictional educational scenario about how a global conflict like World War III could begin. It is designed to help students understand international relations, diplomacy, and the importance of conflict prevention.

Possible Escalation Timeline (Hypothetical)

  • Regional conflict between two nations
  • Military alliances become involved
  • Cyber attacks and economic sanctions increase tensions
  • Large-scale military mobilization
  • Global conflict affecting multiple continents

Potential Global Impact

  • Economic collapse and trade disruption
  • Humanitarian crises and refugee movements
  • Cyber warfare targeting infrastructure
  • Environmental damage

How Wars Can Be Prevented

International diplomacy, organizations like the United Nations, and peaceful negotiations play an important role in preventing global conflicts.
